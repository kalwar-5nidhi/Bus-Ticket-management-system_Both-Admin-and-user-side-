Getting Started ▼

Welcome to our bus booking platform! Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Sign Up: If you're new, click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on the homepage.
  2. Provide Details: Fill out the registration form with your name, email, and password.
  3. Verify Email: Check your email for a verification link and click to confirm your account.
  4. Log In: Once verified, return to the site and log in using your email and password.
  5. Search for Buses: Enter your travel details (origin, destination, dates) on the search form.
  6. Select and Book: Browse available buses, select seats, and proceed to payment to confirm your booking.

Need assistance? Contact our support team for help with registration or booking.

Booking Tickets ▼

Follow these steps to book tickets on our platform:

  1. Search for Buses: On the homepage or booking page, enter your travel details including origin, destination, and travel dates. Click "Search."
  2. View Available Buses: Browse through the list of available buses matching your search criteria. Review schedules, fares, and amenities.
  3. Select Seats: Choose your preferred seats from the seat map displayed for each bus. Seats that are already booked will be marked as unavailable.
  4. Enter Passenger Details: Provide passenger information including names, ages, and contact details.
  5. Proceed to Payment: Review your booking summary, including the total fare and selected seats. Choose a payment method and complete the transaction securely.
  6. Receive Confirmation: Once payment is successful, you will receive a booking confirmation via email or SMS with your ticket details.

For any issues during the booking process, reach out to our customer support for assistance.
